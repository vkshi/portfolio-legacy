  • Senior Project

open in a new tab

For this project, the professor wanted us to graph information on a map. I used Mapbox and datasets from Open Data Philly for resources. I limited my scope to just Philadelphia. For my project, I wanted to compare several things. First, I was interested in the poverty levels of difference districts in Philadelphia. Second, I wanted to compare locations of gun violence to see if they were more frequent or not in areas of high poverty. Last, I also wanted to see if there was a strong correlation between fatalities resulting from gun violence and the racial identities of the victims.
